Carpentry Services Across Mid-Cornwall

Truro's Georgian townhouses, converted chapels and granite-fronted cottages present unique challenges that reward a skilled carpenter. From properties overlooking the Cathedral to countryside homes near Probus and Tresillian, we bring precision joinery to every project in the TR postcode area.

Our work extends to Falmouth, Penryn, Redruth, Perranporth and the villages dotted along the Fal estuary. Cornwall's damp climate and granite construction call for careful timber selection and proven weatherproofing techniques that we have refined over many years.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

RPG Carpentry & Joinery ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal data you share with us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Service you are enquiring about
  • Project details you provide in the message field

We also use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. This collects anonymised data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/region). No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit through our contact form solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Provide a quote for the service you have requested
  • Contact you about your project

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms, a form submission service. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted securely to Web3Forms, which forwards it to our email. Web3Forms processes data in accordance with their own privacy policy. No data is stored permanently on Web3Forms servers.

Our website is hosted on Vercel, which may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal web hosting operations.

Data Retention

We retain your contact form data only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and complete any resulting project. Once your enquiry is resolved, we delete your data unless there is a legitimate business reason to retain it (such as an ongoing project or legal obligation).

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rpgcarpentryjoinery@outlook.com or call 07717 299 777.

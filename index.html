About Us

Devon & Cornwall's Trusted Carpenter

RPG Carpentry & Joinery is a Cornwall-based carpentry and joinery specialist with a passion for exceptional craftsmanship. We work on a wide range of residential and commercial projects across Devon and Cornwall — no job is too small or too large.

Every project is completed to the highest standard, using quality materials and time-honoured techniques combined with modern methods. Our reputation is built on reliability, attention to detail, and work that stands the test of time.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Every project is different, so we provide free, no-obligation quotes tailored to your specific requirements. As a guide, our day rates typically range from £200–£350 depending on the complexity of the work. Larger projects such as barn renovations or full room fits are priced on a project basis after an on-site assessment.

We cover Devon and Cornwall including Plymouth, Exeter, Torquay, Barnstaple, Truro, Tavistock and all surrounding areas. Whether you’re on the coast or inland, we’re happy to travel to assess your project.

Timescales vary depending on the scope. Small jobs like door fitting or skirting typically take 1–3 days. Medium projects such as flooring or fencing run 1–2 weeks. Larger work like barn renovations can take 2–6 months depending on complexity, planning requirements and weather conditions.

Yes, absolutely. We offer free, no-obligation quotes for all work. We’ll visit your property to assess the project in person, discuss your requirements, and provide a detailed written quote within 48 hours. There’s no pressure and no hidden fees.

We work with a wide range of timbers including oak, softwoods (pine, spruce), hardwoods (ash, walnut) and reclaimed timber for character projects. We’ll recommend the best material for your specific project based on durability, aesthetics and budget.

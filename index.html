About Us

Devon & Cornwall's Trusted Carpenter

RPG Carpentry & Joinery is a Cornwall-based carpentry and joinery specialist with a passion for exceptional craftsmanship. We work on a wide range of residential and commercial projects across Devon and Cornwall — no job is too small or too large.

Every project is completed to the highest standard, using quality materials and time-honoured techniques combined with modern methods. Our reputation is built on reliability, attention to detail, and work that stands the test of time.

Our Services

From internal joinery to external structures, we handle every aspect of carpentry with the same care and precision.

🚪

Doors & Frames

Bespoke door fitting and framing for all styles of property, internal and external.

🪟

Windows

Window installation and replacement — maintaining the character of your home.

🌲

Oak Flooring

Expert supply and fitting of solid and engineered oak flooring throughout your home.

🏚️

Barn Renovations

Sensitive restoration and conversion of barns and outbuildings with quality materials.

🏠

Canopies & Pergolas

Handcrafted outdoor canopies and covered structures for beauty and practicality.

🪵

Fencing

Timber fencing solutions for all boundary types — residential and commercial.

📐

Skirting & Boxing

Neat and precise skirting boards, architraves, and pipe boxing to finish any room.

Flat Roofing

Professional flat roof carpentry and decking work as part of wider construction projects.

Bespoke Joinery

Custom-built furniture and joinery — wine racks, shelving, and made-to-measure pieces.
